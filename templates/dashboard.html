Welcome to Neura ‚Äî your futuristic, all-in-one email scheduling platform designed for individuals and small teams who want complete control and seamless automation of their marketing campaigns. This guide will walk you through the essential steps to get started and make the most of the app‚Äôs powerful features.

1. Add a Campaign and Set It Up

Begin by creating a new campaign. Give it a meaningful name and configure its status ‚Äî you can pause or activate the campaign at any time. This forms the foundation of your email outreach.

2. Add a New List and Add Recipients

Next, create a recipient list for your campaign. You can manually add contacts or import them via CSV. Organizing your audience into lists helps you target your emails effectively.

3. Create the Email Content

Compose the email message for your campaign. Use the rich editor to customize your text, add images, and include links. Preview your message before proceeding to ensure it looks perfect.

4. Select the List and Pick the Date

Assign your email to the appropriate recipient list and schedule the sending date and time. You can also choose to send immediately if desired. The scheduling system allows precise control over your campaign timeline.
